Free Web Hosting Sites

If your wanting to build a free website but dont have money to host one this guide will show you how to do that for free with no bs payment methods

W3Spaces - W3Schools Spaces offers a free, cloud-based environment for building and hosting websites, but it has some drawbacks
compared to more established platforms. Pros include ease of use and free hosting, while cons include potential limitations in
features and customizability, and concerns about information accuracy and depth.

GitHub Pages - GitHub Pages is a free service for hosting static websites directly from your GitHub repositories. It's a popular choice
for personal blogs, project documentation, and portfolios due to its ease of use and integration with Git. However, it has limitations,
primarily the inability to handle server-side code or complex web applications

Google Sites - Google Sites offers a simple, free way to build basic websites, particularly advantageous for individuals and small businesses
needing quick, easy online presence. However, it has limitations in customization, SEO, and advanced features, making it less suitable
for complex or e-commerce sites
